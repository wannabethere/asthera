### How Our Agents Think and Learn

Every agentic coworker is powered by our proprietary Self-RAG with Reinforcement Learning system:

#### **1. Retrieval Phase**
- **Intelligent Context Gathering**: Agents search vector databases for relevant information
- **Multi-Source Integration**: Combine internal data, external sources, and domain knowledge
- **Quality Filtering**: Automated relevance scoring and source validation

#### **2. Reasoning Phase** 
- **Step-by-Step Analysis**: Transparent reasoning with intermediate steps
- **Error Detection**: Built-in validation and consistency checking  
- **Confidence Scoring**: Agents know when they're uncertain

#### **3. Generation Phase**
- **Context-Aware Output**: Responses tailored to business context and user needs
- **Quality Validation**: Multi-layer output verification
- **Continuous Learning**: Performance improvement through feedback loops

#### **4. Reinforcement Learning**
- **Outcome Tracking**: Monitor real-world performance of agent outputs
- **Feedback Integration**: Learn from user corrections and preferences
- **Model Improvement**: Continuous fine-tuning based on success metrics
- **Domain Adaptation**: Specialized knowledge for your industry and use cases
