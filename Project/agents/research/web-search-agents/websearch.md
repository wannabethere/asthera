## Overview

Web Search Agents continuously monitor the internet to gather, analyze, and structure information for your business needs. They transform manual research tasks into automated intelligence workflows, keeping your data current and comprehensive.

### Why Web Search Agents?

- **Always-On Intelligence**: Monitor thousands of sources 24/7
- **Real-Time Updates**: Get the latest information as it becomes available
- **Structured Insights**: Convert web data into actionable business intelligence  
- **Scalable Research**: Handle research tasks that would take teams weeks

## 🔍 Featured Search Agents

### Lead Enrichment Agent
**Comprehensive prospect research and data validation**

Transform basic contact information into detailed prospect profiles. This agent researches companies, validates contact details, and enriches records with relevant business intelligence.

**Key Features:**
- Company firmographics and financial data
- Contact verification and social profiles
- Technology stack identification
- News and funding information

```
  - Sarah Johnson (CTO) - LinkedIn verified ✓
  - Mike Chen (Head of Product) - Email validated ✓

```

**Use Cases:**
- Sales prospecting
- Account-based marketing
- Customer onboarding
- Partnership identification
