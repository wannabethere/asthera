Cross-System Analysis Questions
CSOD Training + Workday Skills Development
🔗 INNER JOIN Questions
Questions requiring data from both systems - employees who have both training records AND skills assessments

Training Effectiveness Analysis
"Which training programs have the highest average skill improvement for employees who completed the training and received skills assessments?"
Employee Learning Journey
"Show me the complete learning journey for employees who completed Leadership Basics training - from assignment date through skill proficiency achievement."
ROI Analysis
"What is the return on investment for each training curriculum, comparing training completion time with actual skill development outcomes?"
Manager Team Development
"Which managers have teams that both complete training on time AND achieve target skill proficiency levels?"
Division Performance Correlation
"How does training completion rate in each division correlate with skills proficiency achievement rates?"

🔄 LEFT JOIN Questions
Questions focused on training data with optional skills outcomes

Training Without Skills Assessment
"Which employees completed training programs but haven't received corresponding skills assessments yet?"
Incomplete Learning Cycle
"Show all training completions from the last 6 months and identify which ones are missing follow-up skills evaluations."
Training Volume vs. Skills Development
"For each curriculum, what percentage of completed trainings resulted in measurable skill development?"
Employee Training Portfolio
"List all employees with their complete training history and show which trainings led to documented skill improvements."

➡️ RIGHT JOIN Questions
Questions focused on skills data with optional training history

Skills Development Without Training
"Which employees show skill improvements that aren't linked to any formal training completion in our CSOD system?"
External Learning Impact
"Identify employees with high skill proficiency levels who may have developed skills through external training or experience rather than our internal programs."
Skills Assessment Coverage
"Show all skills assessments and identify which ones are missing corresponding training completion records."

🔗 FULL OUTER JOIN Questions
Questions requiring complete picture from both systems

Comprehensive Learning Analytics
"Create a complete view of our learning ecosystem - show all training activities and all skills assessments, highlighting gaps where data is missing from either system."
Data Quality Assessment
"Identify discrepancies between our training records and skills assessments - where do we have mismatched employee IDs, curriculum titles, or timeline inconsistencies?"

📊 UNION Questions
Questions combining similar data from both systems

Unified Employee Development Timeline
"Create a chronological timeline combining training assignment dates, completion dates, and skills assessment dates for each employee to visualize their complete development journey."
Manager Oversight Dashboard
"Combine training oversight data (assignments, completions, overdue) with skills development oversight (assessments, proficiency gaps) to create a unified manager dashboard."

🔄 Complex Multi-Join Questions
Questions requiring multiple relationships and complex logic

Peer Comparison Analysis
"Compare employees within the same division and job level - who has completed similar training programs but achieved different skill development outcomes?"
Skills Gap Remediation Planning
"For employees with critical skill gaps, what training programs should they be assigned based on what has been most effective for similar employees in achieving target proficiency?"
Succession Pipeline Readiness
"Identify high-performing employees who have completed leadership training AND achieved target proficiency in critical skills, but haven't been assigned to succession planning roles yet."
Training Program Optimization
"Which training programs should we discontinue, modify, or expand based on completion rates, time-to-complete, and actual skill development outcomes across different employee segments?"
